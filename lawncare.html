Lawn Care Basics

Mowing

Mow regularly but never cut more than one‑third of the grass height at once.

Watering

Water early in the morning, 2–3 times per week, to encourage deep root growth.

Fertilizing

Feed your lawn every 6–8 weeks during the growing season for best results.

Weed Control

Remove weeds early and use eco‑friendly weed control solutions when needed.
